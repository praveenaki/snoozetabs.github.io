Snooze Tabs User Guide

Master tab management: snooze tabs for later, save articles to read, build daily routines.

Quick Start

  1. Click the Snooze Tabs icon in your browser toolbar
  2. Pick when you want the tab back - Click a preset (Tomorrow, This Evening, etc.) or "Pick a date & time"
  3. Your tab closes and reappears automatically when the time comes

Features

Quick Snooze Presets

One-click snoozing with 7 preset times: Later Today, This Evening, Tomorrow, and more.

Save for Later

Bookmark tabs without a schedule. Perfect for reference docs you access on-demand.

Custom Date & Time

Schedule tabs for any specific date and time when presets don't fit.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Snooze tabs without opening the popup. Alt+Shift+T for tomorrow, and more.

Recurring Snoozes PRO

Create snoozes that repeat daily, weekly, monthly, or yearly.

Workspaces PRO

Organize snoozes into named categories like Work, Personal, Learning.

Google Drive Sync PRO

Back up and sync your snoozes across devices using Google Drive.

Settings

Customize preset times, notifications, grace periods, and more.

Use Cases

    "I have 50 tabs open and can't think" - Clear the clutter in seconds
  • Research Mode

  • Daily Routines
